DURANDAL (Search and Destroy)

by David Reeves (Hitman)

RED TEAM: Defend your weapon and SAM
crates and destroy the enemy crates.
Target crates have stenciled M60 and
M16 marked on their tops.

BLUE TEAM: You are part of a NATO 
Task Force. Defend your supply crates and
destroy the enemy crates. Your targets
will be marked with FA-MAS and AIM-9
Sidewinder symbols on the top.
