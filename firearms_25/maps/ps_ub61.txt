UB61 By -[DKM]-Gen.Carnage (Territorial push)

Each team must try to capture all check points of the map
in sequence. Once your team captures all check points, all
players respawn, your team's lives increase, and the team gains points

Submarine has capture point inside its controltower, red flag on top of red base bunker.
Blue flag behind netting on top level of blue base bunker.
