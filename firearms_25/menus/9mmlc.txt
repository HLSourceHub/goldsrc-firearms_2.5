9mm-LC

The 9mm-LC, born in the UK, was produced between 1953 and
1988.  This submachine gun holds 35 rounds of 9mm
Parabellum ammunition.  The barrel measures 198mm, and
the gun weighs 2.7kg when empty.  With a rate of fire of
550 rounds per minute, the 9mmlc is a nice addition to any
soldier's arsenal.

Costs 8 credits.
