5.56 AR-GL
The 5.56 AR-GL is the second generation rifle. This weapon
has seen action in several countries and remains the primary
weapon for many armies. The 5.56 AR-GL is a burst / single shot
only rifle that fires 5.56mm, 30 round clips. Attached to the
5.56 AR-GL is a grenade launcher. The AR-GL fires the 40mm
High Explosive Grenade.  The weapon has been modified to fire
at full-auto.

Costs 13 credits.
