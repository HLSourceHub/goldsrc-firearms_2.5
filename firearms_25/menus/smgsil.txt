9mm SMG SIL
The 9mm SMG SIL is the top of the line in close combat
sub-machine guns. Its small size and compactness make this gun
easily concealable. This gun has seen a lot of action when
concealability is needed to maintain cover or to keep the
element of surprise. The 9mm SMG SIL holds a 30 round clip
of 9mm. Attached to the 9mm SMG SIL is a special operations
silencer to maintain the element of surprise during combat.

Costs 8 credits.
